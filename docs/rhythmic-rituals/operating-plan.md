# Rhythmic Ritual ◉ ANIMÆ Operating Plan

## Pre-Campaign Arc

| Phase | Timing | Purpose | Output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Lock | T-8 weeks | Fix venue, date, sound, capacity | private production sheet |
| Signal | T-6 weeks | Place the ◉ and first hidden vector | no explanation post |
| Name | T-5 weeks | Reveal ANIMÆ | poster, website, social kit |
| Lineage | T-4 weeks | Establish sound and trust | music direction post |
| Objects | T-3 weeks | Introduce merch | tee, stamp scarf, token, print |
| Address | T-1 week | Give practical details | venue, entry, timing |
| Trace | T+1 day | Keep the room private but remembered | one photo, one line |

## Night Arc

| Phase | Energy | Sound | Room Mechanic |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Arrival | low signal | minimal warm-up, restrained progressive | stamp or token, no speech |
| Pressure | social focus | patient groove, low swing | room fills, vectors appear |
| Deep Room | locked-in | minimal techno, progressive pressure | floor takes priority |
| ANIMÆ | peak without theatre | harder pulse, precise tension | hidden bestiary mark appears |
| Exit | quiet trace | reduced pressure | merch, token, after-note |

## Rules

- Music first.
- Ceremony stays under the floorboards.
- No literal animals.
- No mascot.
- No forced participation.
- If a line sounds like a festival ad, cut it.
