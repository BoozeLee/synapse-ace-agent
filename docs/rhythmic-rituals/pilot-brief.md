# Pilot Brief: Rhythmic Ritual ◉ — ANIMÆ

## Working Title

Rhythmic Ritual ◉ — ANIMÆ

## Date Window

End August 2026.

## Location

Belgium. Venue to be held back until the campaign needs an address.

## One-Line Pitch

An underground minimal techno night for people who remember enough not to need nostalgia.

## Audience

35-45, Belgian/Dutch underground veterans, and the adjacent crowd that still understands patience.

## Sound

Minimal techno, progressive, late-night pressure, Joris Biesmans lineage.

## Visual System

Abstract vector geometry derived from animal silhouettes. No literal animals. The hidden bestiary is felt, not explained.

## Public Lines

- Not a reunion. A continuation.
- The animals left. The souls stayed.
- Same pulse. Fifteen years wiser.
- No mascot. No cage. No lecture.
