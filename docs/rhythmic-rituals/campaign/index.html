The Format

A real party with an intentional arc.

Rhythmic Rituals is not a wellness workshop with background music, and it is not a normal club night with ceremonial words pasted over it. The ritual layer shapes the room flow, social permission, visuals, and memory of the night.

  • 4 to 7 hour show format
  • 150 to 250 guest pilot capacity
  • Organic house, afro house, melodic techno, percussion, vocals
  • Three optional ritual stations: intention, release, connection
